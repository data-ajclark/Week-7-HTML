There are a number of different utensils and ingredients that are available for purchase to help with your sushi making needs.

Some of the most common are:

  • Bamboo Rolling Mat
  • Sushi Roller
  • Sushi Bazooka
  • Avacado Slicer
  • Sushi Knife
  • Reusable Chopsticks
  • Rice Paddle
  • Rice Spreader
  • Various Sizes of Sushi Molds

Some of the most common ingredients used are:

  • Sushi Rice
  • Nori or most commonly called seaweed
  • Fish or shellfish - either raw or cooked
  • Various vegetables
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
